# OCS download guide

Open the official NHS England
[*Submissions via Online Consultation Systems in General Practice* release
index](https://digital.nhs.uk/data-and-information/publications/statistical/submissions-via-online-consultation-systems-in-general-practice).
Choose the required monthly release, then use its **Resources** section. For
practice-month integration obtain the **CSV zip** and metadata. Obtain the
**Day and Time CSV zip** and its metadata only when performing temporal
analysis.

For the fixed dissertation evidence series, the
[May 2026 OCS release page](https://digital.nhs.uk/data-and-information/publications/statistical/submissions-via-online-consultation-systems-in-general-practice/may-2026)
contains the selected historical practice-level resource. Its notice about
incomplete May 2026 submissions does not make May 2026 part of the fixed April
2025 to March 2026 observation window.

Prefer the most recent reliable publication vintage that contains the required
historical months, but inspect the publication notices for incomplete supplier
submissions or retrospective changes. Record the selected vintage separately
from the analytical observation period.

Validate the practice code, month, supplier, metric and value fields. Registered
patients must be month matched. Supplier non-submission and non-participation
must not be converted to zero submissions.
